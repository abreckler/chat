Blink currently supports sharing natively to messaging platforms like iMessage/WhatsApp/Messenger and broadcast platforms like Twitter/Facebook and others via the apple share sheet.

When you share a ‘blink’ you are sharing the image + a link (back to the blink or to the source app where the blink was originally screen shot).

Our team is working to make sharing a blink even more seamless so that it feels like a native piece of content wherever you choose to share it to.

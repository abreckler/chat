How Blockstack used the Xpo.Network to increase contributor engagement

Blockstack recently started using the Xpo.Network to support community growth. The challenge with an open community network is identifying active users that add value to the eco-system. With the Xpo.Network, community members earn rewards through verified, proof-of-action campaigns. That means rewards go the users that complete the listed tasks. There is no uncertainty at any point of the value chain.

Xpo-nential Results

  • To-date, more than 600 contributors have participated in the blockstack community rewards program.
  • Over 160k tokens have been earned on the platform for various tasks from bug fixes to tutorials, blog posts and everything in between.

Projects > Tasks and Rewards

Incentivizing participation has led to an increase in engagement with users. By empowering contributors and encouraging them to become shareholders and feedback voices, this program has helped Blockstack take giant strides. In the words of Blockstack co-founder, Ryan Shea:

While this tweet wouldn’t have earned him any tokens, one sharing the registration page for the Universal Wallet would have netted him 30 stacks valued at approximately $4.50.

Some of the most rewarded tasks include:

  • Creating tutorials for blockstack.js developer tools (link)
  • Messaging decentralized app creators with feedback and feature requests or bug notifications (link)
  • Holding an event or conference that features a member of the Blockstack ecosystem (link)

Users can earn tokens for everything from writing blog posts to creating step-by-step tutorials. There are even opportunities to organize local events or use supported apps, like Graphite. Even feedback has a place in the rewards structure.

Developers can help with eslint compliance by fulfilling one of the pull requests posted on Github. Participants earn 100 stacks for every removed source file.

Proof of Community Engagement

By also looping in social media, Blockstack is able to extend the reach of their platform, with all of the engagement managed from a single platform. Decentralization may be the eventual goal of blockchain technologies, but centralized data collection adds a lot of value to their current marketing efforts.

These tweets by Blockstack supporters are just a few examples of rewarded communications that also show the value of the technology.

Transparency and Automation

Part of what makes our Xpo.Network so valuable as an engagement building tool is transparency. The reward for each type of action is clearly listed on the community rewards page, so there is no confusion about expectations and completion.

The Xpo.Network helps blockchain-based projects create an open network of contributors who are all rewarded for their activity. Instead of building users, we help build a platform that turns users into stakeholders. Stakeholders are intrinsically more invested in the success of the technology, and will spend more time developing the community.

With our help, Blockstack has grown tremendously and will continue to reach out to leading voices in the blockchain development community.
